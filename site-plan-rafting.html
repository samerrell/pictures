Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to infrom people on how fun and adventurous rafting can be. Also it's meant to encourage and spark interest in people who have thought about rafting. Much info is given so that people know how rafting works, when they can sign up and where the rafting will take place.

Audience

Anyone and everyone that aren't afraid of water and love adventure. Rafting is a fun way to conect so, if friend groups want to have a fun time, go rafting. Rafting is meant for family's, groups, friends, camps or just for fun! Anyone can do it, just as long ag you can wear a life jacket and are ok with the thought of ending up in the water.
